About ES Mukingi

Ecole Secondaire MUKINGI (ESM) yashinzwe mu 1996 i Ruhango, Byimana. Ni ishuri ryiyemeje gutanga uburezi bufite ireme, rishingiye ku bumenyi, indangagaciro n’ikoranabuhanga.

  • Founded: 1996
  • Location: Ruhango – Byimana
  • Motto: URISHYIRIRAHO WOWE
  • Curriculum ya NESA

School Anthem
